THE FALL


The Fall was an English post-punk band from Manchester, UK. Fronted by Mark E. Smith, he remained the only constant member for their over 40 year tenure. Charactarized by his unique stream of consciousness ramblings and volatile songwriting styles, they are considered one of the most unique and infuential groups in the underground music world. Take a more comprehensive dive into their history at The Fall's Wikipedia page.


Some classic records (of their prolific catalog)

  • Dragnet (1979)
  • Hex Enduction Hour (1982)
  • The Wonderful and Frightening World Of...(1984)
  • Bend Sinister (1986)
